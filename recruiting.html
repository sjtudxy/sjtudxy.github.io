For Prospective Students:


  • First/Second-year Ph.D. students:
      If you are looking for a thesis advisor, feel free to email me.

  • PhD Applicants:
    • I am looking for highly self-motivated PhD students with funding support. If you are interested in working with me, please send an email xinya.du@utdallas.edu (with title: [Prospective PhD Student - Spring 2023 or Fall 2023]) including:
      • Your CV/Resume
      • Your transcript (both undergrad and higher)
      • Your representative work (e.g., publication, thesis, research project and repo)
      • A description of your research interests, and future career plan (Optional).
      We accept applications with flexible deadlines, the program starting date includes both Spring 2023 and Fall 2023. Please refer to here for general application information from the CS deparment. Due to large amount of applications, please mention my name in your personal statement.

  • Master or Undergrad Students (in or outside UTD):
    • Other students passionate about research are also welcome to contact me, especially you can consistently commit time to research. Having good engineering skills is extra bonus. Please drop me an email (with title: [Prospective Intern/Master/Internship Student]) including:
      • CV (including your experiences) and transcript (unofficial, both undergrad and higher)
      • Time you plan to commit each week/month
      • Your plan after graduation (e.g., industry positions or applying for PhD program)
